Challenge

  • Airport Taxi in Nigeria has been challenged with the issues of poor system for a long time.
  • Passenger touting due to bad taxi orgainsation.
  • Revenue loss for the airport managemnt
  • Insecurity because Drivers were not properly documented by the airport system

Solution
