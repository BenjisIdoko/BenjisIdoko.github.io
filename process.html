My Process

How i get work Done

1: Understand

I believe interacting with the product owner and the end-user is key to succesful product, this helps me to get clarity of their business needs or unique situation. Generally its like dating the client just to know them better

  • Clearly Listen
  • Define Challenges
  • Understand the Users
  • Define requiremnts
  • Create User Personas

2: Analyze

After obtaining a clear understanding of the product and the users i attempt to create a solution map, I move onto researching the market, analyzing competitors and exploring possible solutions. I want to learn as much about the competitive landscape as possible. In addition, I look at apps across the digital landscape that may bring ideas and UI design solutions. With these information gathered, I begin sketching ideas, and defining user task flows

  • Brainstorm ideas
  • Market research
  • Analyze competitive landscape
  • Define task flows
  • Create wireframes

3: Visual Design

After exploring potential ideas, defining the user flows, structuring the wireframes, I begin the process of visual UI design of the low-fidelity screens. This includes defining the initial elements that will be used to create the overall design system. During the process of designing each of the essential screens, I’ll define the colors, typography, iconography, images, illustrations and overall design language.

  • Sketch, Figma or Adobe XD
  • Define overall design language
  • Design essential screens
  • Design UI elements
  • Create prototypes
  • Create style guide

4: Hand Off

Once the designs are final and have been approved, I establish a communication link with the engineers and keep the communication open throughout the development process.I have been using zeplin as my handoff tool for the past three years because of the options available and useful for developers.

  • Hand-off to engineers
  • Product demonstrations
  • Collaborate, assist and monitor

6: Test & Iterate

Just Before signing off and launching a digital product into production, I believe in getting the product in front of users - early interactive prototypes or production ready demos; just to be we have achieved our set out goal and solved all necessary problems and I not we iterate.
